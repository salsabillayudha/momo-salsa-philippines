Good to know

Visas

Indonesian passport: visa-free 30 days. Momo: check requirements for your passport nationality — UAE residency alone doesn't grant entry, the passport does.

Flights

Manila → El Nido is small AirSwift planes with tight baggage limits — book early and pack light. The 8 Dec Coron → Bohol day routes via Manila; leave a comfortable connection.

Cash

El Nido and Coron are largely cash; Coron ATMs run dry in peak season. Load up on pesos in Manila before flying out.

Peak season

December fills up fast. Book the El Nido resort, the El Nido–Coron ferry, and all flights well ahead.

Connectivity

Grab a local eSIM or Globe/Smart SIM at MNL. Signal is fine in town, patchy out on the boats.

Seas & tours

Late-Nov Palawan seas can occasionally be choppy — a tour may reschedule. Keep one flexible day per island.
